About me

Designer focused on clarity, systems, and human-centered digital experiences.

I’m a product and UX designer with over a decade of experience working across digital products, platforms, and brand ecosystems.

My background spans advertising, visual design, and UX, which allows me to move fluidly between strategy, structure, and execution. I care deeply about clarity — in interfaces, in systems, and in decision-making.

I’ve worked with multidisciplinary teams, translating complex requirements into accessible, scalable solutions that balance user needs and business goals.

How I work

  • Research-led, not assumption-driven
  • Strong focus on information architecture and flows
  • Design systems as living tools, not static artifacts
  • Close collaboration with engineering and product
  • Accessibility as a baseline, not a feature

What I care about

I’m drawn to projects where design has real impact: education, fintech, tools that support well-being, and products that reduce cognitive load instead of adding to it.

I value thoughtful teams, honest conversations, and work that prioritizes long-term quality over short-term polish.
